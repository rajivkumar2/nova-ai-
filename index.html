FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Is there a free trial? Do I need a credit card?
Yes! Nova AI offers a 14-day free trial on all plans with full access to every feature. No credit card is required to start — just sign up and begin. We'll remind you before your trial ends.
Which apps and tools does Nova AI integrate with?
Nova AI integrates with 50+ popular tools including Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, Slack, Notion, Trello, Asana, HubSpot, Salesforce, Zapier, and more. Our API also allows custom integrations for Enterprise plans.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely. Nova AI is SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and uses end-to-end AES-256 encryption. Your data is never sold or used to train AI models. Enterprise plans include zero-data-retention options.
Can I cancel or switch plans anytime?
Yes, completely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you retain access until the end of your billing period. No hidden fees or cancellation charges.
How is Nova AI different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT is a general AI chatbot. Nova AI is a purpose-built productivity operating system that connects to your actual tools, learns your context, remembers your preferences, and takes autonomous actions — like booking meetings, sending emails, and running research — on your behalf.
What kind of support do you offer?
Starter users get email support within 24 hours. Pro users get priority chat support within 2 hours. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager, onboarding sessions, and 24/7 phone support.
